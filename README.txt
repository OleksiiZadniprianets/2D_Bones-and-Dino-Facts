      Bones & Dino Facts is a simple educational mini-game developed in Unity where the player learns about dinosaurs by matching facts with the correct skeletons. During the game, a fact appears on the screen, and the player must choose the correct dinosaur by dragging its skeleton into the answer area. After each attempt, the game shows whether the answer was correct and reveals the real appearance of the dinosaur, allowing the player to better remember both the visual and factual information.

      The game consists of three difficulty levels that gradually increase the complexity of the facts, making the experience more challenging and engaging over time. All answers across the levels are combined into a final score, giving the player a clear understanding of their overall performance. The project combines simple drag-and-drop mechanics with visual feedback and background music, creating an interactive and enjoyable way to learn interesting facts about dinosaurs.
